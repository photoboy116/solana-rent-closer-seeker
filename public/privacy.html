Rent Closer Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 30, 2026

Overview

Rent Closer is a client-side Solana utility that helps users find empty token accounts and submit close-account transactions through their connected wallet.

Data We Collect

Rent Closer does not run a backend server and does not intentionally collect, sell, or share personal information. The app may read public blockchain data associated with the wallet address that the user connects.

Wallet Data

Wallet addresses, token account addresses, transaction signatures, and account balances are public Solana blockchain data. The app uses this data to display closable token accounts and prepare transactions. Private keys and seed phrases are never requested, stored, or transmitted by Rent Closer.

Third-Party Services

The app connects to Solana RPC infrastructure and wallet software selected by the user. Those services may process requests according to their own privacy policies.
