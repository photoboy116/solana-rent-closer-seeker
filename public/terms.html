Use of the App

Rent Closer is a non-custodial Solana utility that helps users prepare transactions for closing empty token accounts. You are responsible for reviewing all transaction details before approving them in your wallet.

No Custody or Financial Advice

Rent Closer does not custody assets, manage private keys, or provide financial, investment, tax, or legal advice. Blockchain transactions are irreversible.

Wallet Security

Never share your seed phrase or private key. Rent Closer will never ask for either. Transactions must be signed by your connected wallet.
